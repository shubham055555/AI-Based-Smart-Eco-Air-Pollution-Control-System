## 📌 Problem Description

Air pollution has become a major environmental and health issue affecting both urban and semi-urban regions. A significant portion of pollution originates from specific high-emission sources such as industrial chimneys, enclosed indoor spaces, commercial zones, and poorly ventilated environments.

Most existing air purification systems focus on cleaning air **after pollution has already spread**, making them inefficient, expensive, and unsuitable for outdoor or large-scale deployment. These systems also rely on synthetic filters, which are not environmentally sustainable.

Additionally, traditional systems lack:
- ❌ Source-level pollution control  
- ❌ Real-time intelligent decision-making  
- ❌ Scalability for different environments  

This creates a strong need for a **cost-effective, eco-friendly, and intelligent system** capable of controlling pollution directly at its source.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Reduce air pollution at the **source level**
- Develop a **low-cost and eco-friendly solution**
- Enable **indoor + outdoor deployment**
- Monitor air quality in **real-time**
- Automate purification based on pollution levels
- Integrate **AI-based decision-making**
- Promote **sustainable filtration (Bamboo + Charcoal)**
- Ensure scalability and adaptability

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

We propose a **Smart AI-enabled Air Pollution Control System** that actively reduces pollution at its source rather than reacting after dispersion.

### 🔍 Key Working:

- Sensors continuously monitor:
  - PM2.5 (Particulate Matter)
  - Harmful gases (MQ135)
  - Temperature & Humidity (DHT11)

- Data is processed using an **embedded system (Arduino UNO)**

- AI-based logic:
  - Detects pollution patterns  
  - Predicts pollution levels  
  - Optimizes purification response  

- When pollution exceeds threshold:
  - System automatically activates purification

The system uses:

- 🎋 **Bamboo**
- 🌑 **Charcoal**

- Absorb harmful pollutants  
- Are biodegradable  
- Reduce environmental impact
